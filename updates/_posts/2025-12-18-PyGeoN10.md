We released version 1.0 of [PyGeoN](https://github.com/compgeo-mox/pygeon) today. This release contains
- A more unified structure between the discretization classes and their handling of data dicitonaries.
- Quadratic finite elements, including second-order Lagrange and the Raviart-Thoma $\mathbb{RT}_1$.
- Tensor-valued elements for mixed formulations of (Cosserat) elasticity. 
- Revised tutorials.
- Improved testing suite using pytest, with full coverage.
